Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join Gyanhive Institute?

Any A-level student seeking academic excellence and a creative, supportive environment is welcome to apply. We offer online and home-to-home tutoring for maximum flexibility.

What are the class timings?

Class timings are flexible and scheduled to suit students' needs, with both online and home-to-home options.

Are notes and materials provided?

Yes, comprehensive notes and materials are provided for all A-level subjects.

Is there a trial class?

We offer introductory sessions for prospective students to experience our online or home-to-home tutoring.

How do I enroll?

Contact us via the website to start your enrollment process for online or home-to-home tutoring.
